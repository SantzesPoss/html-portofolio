About Me


Hello! My name is Thanasis, and I am a former Naval Officer eager to embark on a new career filled with exciting challenges.


I was born and raised in Athens, Greece, where I developed a passion for music and gymnastics. During my teenage years, I played water polo, and my love for swimming continued during my time at the Naval Academy. There, I was a proud member of the swimming team, competing in tournaments and specializing in the 100m butterfly.


Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Here, you'll discover my educational background, professional experiences, technical skills, and personal projects. I look forward to connecting and exploring potential collaborations.


My journey began with a strong academic foundation, achieving a 92.5% pass rate in the Panhellenic exams in 2007, which granted me access to all Greek universities. I chose to pursue my studies at the Naval Academy, seeking the discipline necessary to fulfill many of my ambitions. I graduated as an ensign in 2011.


Following my bachelor's degree, which I completed in four years (within the five-year deadline), I served as a naval officer in the Hellenic Navy for over 15 years with a prestigious ten-year period in submarine service, taking on numerous positions of responsibility that demonstrated my reliability in serious roles. My roles included executive officer on a gunboat, operations manager, weapons stewardship manager and navigation officer.
